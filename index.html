Deep Dive · Case Study

AI Sales Forecasting Suite
(MAPE <30%)

A real-world predictive analytics tool built for a local Bristol bakery — turning 10+ years of messy transaction data into actionable inventory intelligence.

01

The Challenge

A Bristol bakery was over-ordering perishable goods weekly, generating waste and eroding margins. Their transaction records spanned 10+ years but were inconsistent, incomplete, and entirely manual.

02

The Solution

I architected a PyQt5 desktop app with a custom data preprocessing engine. I chose Prophet over SARIMA for its ability to handle holiday effects and missing data without manual tuning — critical for a bakery with irregular trading hours.

03

The Result

A comparative ML engine (Prophet + SARIMA + Random Forest) achieving MAPE <30% for high-loss perishable goods. Processing latency held at <0.15s for datasets over 10 years. 100% data integrity maintained across automated imputation pipelines.

Why These Technical Choices?

Prophet over SARIMA SARIMA requires stationary data and manual parameter tuning (p, d, q). Prophet handles trends, seasonality, and holiday effects out of the box — essential when the bakery had inconsistent opening hours and seasonal demand spikes that SARIMA would misread as noise.
PyQt5 over Web Dashboard A web dashboard would require the bakery owner to maintain a server or subscription. A standalone PyQt5 app runs locally, works offline, and protects customer transaction data — aligning with GDPR localization requirements without adding IT overhead.
Hybrid Water-Scrum-Fall Pure Agile struggled with our fixed academic deadline. Pure Waterfall was too rigid for changing requirements. A hybrid model gave us sprint flexibility within a fixed delivery structure — we completed 15 sprints while meeting every deadline.
Automated Imputation over Manual Cleaning Manual CSV cleaning would have taken weeks and introduced human error. I built automated pipelines in Pandas with statistical imputation, reducing preprocessing time to minutes and ensuring reproducibility across every model run.

Primary Prediction Interface

28-day forecasts visualised against actual transaction volume with seasonal decomposition. The dual-line view lets the owner spot divergence early and adjust orders before waste occurs.

Model Evaluation Panel

MAE: 14.58 · RMSE: 20.94

Built-in diagnostics so the owner always knows how much to trust today's suggestion — not just what it says.

Trend Analysis

Daily peaks and troughs identified to optimise staff scheduling and reduce waste during low-demand windows.

Inventory Scaling

A dynamic view for the owner to adjust supply orders based on AI-predicted demand — no data science knowledge required.
